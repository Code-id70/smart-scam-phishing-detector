      Common Phishing Tactics:
      • Urgency: Messages that pressure you to act immediately
      • Threats: Claims of account suspension or legal action
      • Too Good to Be True: Unsolicited prizes or rewards
      • Impersonation: Pretending to be from trusted organizations
      • Suspicious Links: Shortened URLs or unusual domains

      Remember: Legitimate organizations never ask for sensitive information via unsolicited messages.
